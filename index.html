MQ Leveling Strategy

⚡
Skip Early Bosses with MQ
Doing the Main Quest to defeat Finstern can take you from Lv.1 to Lv.75 in minutes — vs hours of early-game boss grinding. Always do MQ first to skip the low-EXP early stretch.
📖
Chapters 9–13 = Gold
Completing MQ chapters 9, 10, 11, and 12 gives roughly 25% of the EXP needed to go from Lv.240 to Lv.270. The final quest alone gives 112,000,000 EXP. Preserve these on alt characters!
🔄
Start from Latest (Only for New Accounts)
After clearing Chapter 8 (Finstern), create new characters and select "Start from Latest" to skip straight to Chapter 9 and preserve all the big EXP chapters for them too.
📅
Adventurer's Diaries
These repeatable quest items give great EXP. The calculator on this tab lets you calculate how many diary runs you need alongside MQ. Use them to bridge EXP gaps between boss brackets.

Guild Tips & Best Practices

🍽
Optimal Buff Stack
You can hold 4 food buff effects simultaneously. For bossing, aim for:
  • ATK or MATK food (matched to your build)
  • Elemental drink matching boss weakness
  • HP food (Roasted Lamb for tanks)
  • EXP Book if leveling
🐾
Pet Leveling Rules
  • Pets get 0 EXP if they die — keep them alive!
  • Use Easy mode boss for safe pet grinding
  • EXP Books apply to pets too — use them
  • Pets can't benefit from quest EXP, only kills
  • Stack pet on top of character leveling to double efficiency
👥
Party vs Solo
Full 4-player parties give a drop rate boost that mercenaries and pets don't. For bossing EXP runs, always shout "lfp" (looking for party) in shout chat at your leveling spot. Guild parties = best EXP/hour.
🔒
Story Lock Bosses
Many bosses are story-locked — if a boss isn't spawning at a location, you need to progress the Main Quest first. Complete MQ chapters to unlock high-EXP spots like Venena, Finstern, and Espectro.
⚗
Alchemy for Consumables
Level the Alchemy skill to craft your own Revita, Medicine, and attack consumables. This saves spina long-term. Craft in bulk before boss runs so you never run out mid-fight.
🏠
Cooking System (Your Land)
Cooked food lasts 12 hours and is stronger than dropped food buffs. With a Refrigerator, you can pre-cook a second dish. The same recipe levels up over time — max level 10 gives much better stats. Always cook before long grind sessions.
💰
Spina from Grinding
Save valuable drops like Minotaur Skins and boss materials — sell on the Consignment Board. Early to mid-game drops often sell well. Never vendor unique boss drops without checking market price first.
